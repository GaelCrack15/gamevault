Our Vision

What can you expect from GameVault?

GameVault is a website where users can look up the games they're interested in, keave reviews and interact with the community. If you can't find the game you're looking for, why not create it's page yourself?

A library from the community, for the community.

  • Wanted, finished or in progress?

    In GameVault you can tag games as wanted, in progress or finished.

  • Speak your mind!

    We developed a review system so you and others can rate the games and speak about them.

Our Mission

GameVault's objective is to make it easy for gamers to manage their videogame collection, as well as the interactions with other players that like the same genre, creating tighter communities. By being able to manage a person's games, no matter their device, and creating a web app that centralizes this information, we hope that it will be used as a safe and efficient space to interact with people of similar interests.

Features

This are some of the features of our website:

Account system

GameVault counts with an user account system. You will always be able to come back and still have your games saved.

Open addition system

If you can't find the game you are looking for, you can add it by filling out a simple form.

User profiles

Once you have an account, you can customize your profile picture, username and the games you like.

Reviews

As a logged-in user, you can leave reviews for the games you love (or in any case, despise).

Frecuently Asked Questions

This are some questions most of the users have:

  • How much does it cost to use the website?

    You don't need to pay for anything. All of our features are free and available to every user.

  • What happens if I don't find the game i was looking for?

    If you can't find a game that you're interested in, you can add it yourself by clicking your profile picture in the main page, then click add a game.

  • How many games can i save in my library?

    You can save as many as you like in your library.

  • Can I interact in any way with the site without an account?

    If someone sends you a link to a game page, you'll be able to see it but won't be able to leave reviews or save the game in a library.

  • How does the library work?

    You can see the games you've saved in your profile page. You will also be able to delete them, edit their status or go to their page.

  • What happens to my account after I delete it?

    After you delete your account, all of information will be deleted. This includes your password, all the info tied to your account and your library.
